NodeSchool Lodz - Idea

NodeSchool is an open project of free workshops organized to help people learn Node.js and web software skills. It is aimed both for programmers who know nothing about Node.js and for Node.js programmers who want to learn new things.

NodeSchool Lodz is an open initiative. It was started by goodylabs and has received support from companies like GFT Poland, Comarch, Binar::Apps or Sii Poland.

The next meeting will take place on 23 th November 2019 at 10AM. We meet in the Sii Office at Kopcińskiego Street 79. The Introduction to Node.js workshop will be led by Krzysztof Zbiciński and Mateusz Pokora.
